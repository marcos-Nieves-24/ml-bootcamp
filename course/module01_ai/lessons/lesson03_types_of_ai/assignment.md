# Assignment 3: AI Type Analysis Report

## Objectives

- Apply the AI classification frameworks to real systems
- Critically evaluate AI capabilities versus claims
- Research and synthesize information from multiple sources
- Develop a structured argument about AI types

## Instructions

Select **three** AI systems from different domains (e.g., one in healthcare, one in finance, one in entertainment). For each system, write a detailed analysis (300-400 words per system) covering:

### Section 1: System Overview
- What is the system? Who built it? What does it do?
- What technology does it use (ML, deep learning, rules)?

### Section 2: Classification
- By capability: Narrow AI, AGI, or ASI? Justify.
- By functionality: Reactive, Limited Memory, or beyond? Justify.
- What sensors/percepts does it use?
- What actuators/actions does it produce?

### Section 3: Limitations
- What can this system NOT do?
- What would be needed to make it more general?
- How does it fail?

### Section 4: Future Potential
- Could this system be part of an AGI? How?
- What would improving this system teach us about AI?

## Deliverables

- PDF or Markdown document
- 900-1200 words total
- At least 3 cited references
- Include a table comparing your three systems

## Evaluation Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent (4) | Good (3) | Adequate (2) | Poor (1) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Classification Accuracy** | All three systems classified correctly and justified | 2 of 3 correct | 1 of 3 correct | None correct |
| **Depth of Analysis** | Deep, insightful analysis of capabilities and limitations | Good analysis | Surface-level | Superficial |
| **Critical Evaluation** | Thoughtful critique of AI claims | Some critique | Little critique | None |
| **Structure & Clarity** | Clear, well-organized writing | Mostly clear | Somewhat disorganized | Poor |
| **References** | 3+ quality sources, correct APA | 3 sources | 2 sources | 1 or fewer |
